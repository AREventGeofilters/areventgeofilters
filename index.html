What is a geofilter?

Geofilters provide a fun way for you to share where you are, or what you're up to, by adding an overlay to your snaps (photos taken on the app). Snapchat now allows users and businesses to create their own custom geofilters for a small charge. The larger the area and longer the duration, the higher the cost. On average, Snapchat charges roughly $20 a day.
